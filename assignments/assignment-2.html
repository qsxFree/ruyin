Mobile-based Food Ordering Platform for Daet Pabili

Description

Mobile-based Food Ordering Platform for Daet Pabili is an application where costumer could avail and search services, orders and transaction Daet Pabili offers, where you could relax while waiting for your delivery to come and pay other to accomplish your task.

Target Audience

The target audience are all of the costumers living besides Daet Camarines Norte and also people near the town. The fees and charges are dependent on the customer’s location from the home base which is at Daet. And because of today’s pandemic most of the people use online mode transaction as alternative of not going out of their houses, making Daet Pabili to have a more number of target audience.

Features


Customer Side

  • Menu and Establishment Browsing
  • Browsing other services Daet Pabili offers
  • Basic Food Ordering
  • Item Rating and feedback
  • Different secure payment method
  • Delivery Tracking
  • Chat Service
  • Account for User

Operator Side

  • Order Tracking
  • Order Management
  • Item Management
  • Rating management for data analyzation
  • User Account control
  • Sales Management
  • Nearest Delivery personnel Tracking
  • Delivery personnel chat service

Business History
