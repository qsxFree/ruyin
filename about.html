About IT 116

IT 116 or System Analysis and Design is computer-related course that deals with methodologies, solution designing, concept, theories, team management for System Analyst. This course is the first step for a student to pursue the career of being a system analyst. This is also an essential discipline for an IT-person in order for them to implement a computer-related solution for a business problem.
